OUR WORK

PROVIDE BASIC KNOWLEDGE

We provide the basics of calligraphy, so that everyone can easily study and practice by themselves. This knowledge is suitable for all ages, all professions and cultures.

PRESERVING CALLIGRAPHY VALUES

Calligraphy is an extremely precious cultural value of humanity, our activities aim to increase the number of calligraphy lovers and help it be passed down to the next generations.

CREATING A COMMUNITY OF CALLIGRAPHY

A community that loves calligraphy is very important, where you can learn from better people, have a chance to show your abilities and voice. We have been connecting calligraphy lovers to create such a community.
